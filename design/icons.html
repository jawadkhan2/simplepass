SimplePass — icon concepts

Send links, files & 1:1 chat between devices. Pick one number; I wire it into the app (favicon, header, Tauri icon).

1Orbit Send

Paper plane inside a ring — sending + a closed loop between two devices.

2Handoff

Two mirrored chevrons facing in — data passing between peers. Minimal, scales tiny.

3Chat Link

Speech bubble holding a link glyph — covers chat + link/file sharing in one mark.

4Quick Bolt

Rounded app tile with a lightning bolt — speed of an instant pass. Strong at favicon size.

5Peer Arrow

Two device nodes with an arrow between — literal 1:1 transfer. Reads clearly at any size.

6Node P

Five nodes wired into a "P" — SimplePass monogram built from the network graph itself.

7Pulse Link

Two nodes with a live signal pulse between them — transfer in motion, not just a static line.
